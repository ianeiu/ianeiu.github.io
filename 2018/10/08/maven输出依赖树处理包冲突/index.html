  2. 搜索 asm，在相应父包增加排除

    <dependency>
    <groupId>org.springframework.data</groupId>
    <artifactId>spring-data-hadoop</artifactId>
    <version>2.5.0.RELEASE</version>
    <exclusions>
        <exclusion>
            <artifactId>asm</artifactId>
            <groupId>asm</groupId>
        </exclusion>
    </exclusions>
</dependency>
